Welcome to OurDocs!

OurDocs strives to be the easiest and fastest way to collaborate with others on the same document. If there are any areas you think could be improved or would like additional features added, feel free to email me at translucency@outlook.com.

Let's get started! Here's how:

  1. Click the menu button at the top left and add a new document.
  2. Copy the permanent, unique link to your notebook at the top right.
    • Save this link! It's how you'll access your notebook later.
  3. Share the link with someone and start collaborating!

Hint: You can add as many documents as you like! Your partner(s) will be able to see all of them.
